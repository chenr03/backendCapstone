GET /players

GET /players/:playerId

Gets a single player by his/ her playerId inside the path parameter

POST /players

Creates a player by inserting into the table, either one of the path parameters, such as; playerId, playerName, gameId, Hole1Score, Hole2Score, Hole3Score, Hole4Score, Hole5Score, Hole6Score, Hole7Score, Hole8Score, Hole9Score, Hole10Score, Hole11Score, Hole12Score, Hole13Score, Hole14Score, Hole15Score, Hole16Score, Hole17Score, Hole18Score.

DELETE /players/:playerId

Deletes a player by his / her playerId

PUT /players/:playerId

Updates players inside the AllPlayers' table by either their playerId, playerName, gameId, Hole1Score, Hole2Score, Hole3Score, Hole4Score, Hole5Score, Hole6Score, Hole7Score, Hole8Score, Hole9Score, Hole10Score, Hole11Score, Hole12Score, Hole13Score, Hole14Score Hole15Score, Hole16Score, Hole17Score, and Hole18Score all at once or individually that is up to the client.
